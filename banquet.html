Cascade Conferencing and Banquet Facilities

We have well-equipped, fully functional conference and banqueting facilities to cater to a wide range of indoor activities, including corporate events, private occasions, weddings, and more.

Kahawa Hall

Our largest conference room, Kahawa Hall, is conveniently located at Cascade Plaza's First Floor. It is equipped with state-of-the-art meeting equipment and supplies, offering features such as wireless high-speed Internet connectivity throughout the facility, a smart screen, audio and video recording of sessions, global videoconferencing capabilities (both uplink and downlink), as well as lighting and special effects.

Shangwe Hall

Nestled on the first floor at the far end of the property, Shangwe Hall provides a tranquil and adaptable space that encourages brainstorming in a more relaxed setting. This hall is equipped with a smart screen, enabling you to project materials directly from your laptop or mobile device. With its bright and airy atmosphere, it is an ideal space for mid-sized meetings.

Ukarimu Hall

Ukarimu Hall, situated on the first floor of the historic part of the property, boasts floor-to-ceiling windows and a smart screen for seamless projection from a laptop or other mobile device. This room is well-suited for various gatherings, including meetings, executive conferences, and social events, thanks to its inviting ambiance.
